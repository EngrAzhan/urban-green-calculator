Carbon & Fuel Theory

This page explains the core formulas used by the calculator and the sources for default values (Karachi-based).

Key Equations

  1. Fuel per day (L) = Fuel efficiency (L/h) × Operating hours (h/day)
  2. CO₂ per day (kg) = Fuel per day × CO₂ factor (kg CO₂/L). Default = 2.68 kg CO₂/L (IPCC)
  3. Diesel energy (kWh/day) = Generator power (kW) × Operating hours (h/day)
  4. Solar energy (kWh/day) = Solar capacity (kW) × Sun hours (h/day)
  5. Annual CO₂ saved (kg) = CO₂ per day × 365 × min(solarEnergy/dieselEnergy,1)
  6. Payback (years) = Installation cost / Annual savings

Default Karachi values and sources

  • Diesel price: 285 PKR/L (PSO retail prices, 2024)
  • Sun hours: 5.5 h/day (NREL / AEDB datasets)
  • CO₂ factor: 2.68 kg CO₂/L (IPCC)
  • Fuel efficiency (example): 2.2 L/h for a 10 kW genset (manufacturer curves)

Notes on assumptions

All assumptions are cited in the report. The calculator uses simple engineering approximations for clarity and teaching purposes.
