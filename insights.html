Theoretical Insights

Scientific displacement logic and mitigation frameworks.

Folio No. 001

Diesel Combustion Mechanics

Analysis of decentralized diesel generators reveals a direct output of $CO_2$, $NO_x$, and $PM_{2.5}$, significantly degrading localized air quality in Karachi.

Folio No. 002

The Energy Gap Crisis

Grid instability forces a shift to carbon-intensive backup power. This systemic energy gap is the primary driver for decentralized emission growth.

Folio No. 003

Photovoltaic Displacement

Solar integration functions by displacing thermal loads. With Karachi's 5.5 peak sun hours, carbon debt reduction is calculated linearly.

Folio No. 004

Biological Sequestration

A mature tree acts as a biological carbon sink, absorbing approximately 22kg of $CO_2$ annually through photosynthesis.

Folio No. 005

Urban Heat Mitigation

Strategic urban forestry counteracts the "Urban Heat Island" effect, providing cooling through evapotranspiration.

Folio No. 006

Net Zero Methodology

A zero-sum balance is achieved through aggressive source reduction (Solar) combined with atmospheric removal (Urban Forestry).
