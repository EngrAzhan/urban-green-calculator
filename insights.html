Theoretical Insights

Technical documentation regarding carbon displacement and mitigation frameworks.

FOLIO NO. 001

Diesel Combustion Mechanics

Analysis of decentralized diesel generators reveals a direct output of $CO_2$, $NO_x$, and $PM_{2.5}$, which significantly degrades localized air quality in Karachi.

FOLIO NO. 002

The Load Shedding Energy Gap

Grid instability forces a shift to carbon-intensive backup power. This systemic energy gap is the primary driver for decentralized emission growth in urban centers.

FOLIO NO. 003

Photovoltaic Displacement Logic

Solar energy integration functions by displacing the thermal load of generators. With Karachi's 5.5 peak sun hours, the carbon debt reduction is calculated linearly.

FOLIO NO. 004

Biological Carbon Sequestration

A mature tree acts as a biological carbon sink, absorbing approximately 22kg of $CO_2$ annually through the natural process of photosynthesis.

FOLIO NO. 005

Urban Heat Mitigation

Strategic urban forestry counteracts the "Urban Heat Island" effect, providing localized cooling through evapotranspiration and shaded surface areas.

FOLIO NO. 006

Net Zero Mathematical Roadmap

The path to carbon neutrality is a hybrid balance: aggressive source reduction via Solar PV combined with biological removal via Urban Forestry.
