Urban Trees & Carbon Sequestration

This page summarizes tree species and sequestration values compiled from local studies (Bano et al., Shehzad et al., Pakistan Journal of Botany) specifically for the climate of Karachi.

Calculating Green Mitigation

To find out how many trees you need to offset your emissions:
Formula: Total Annual CO₂ (from Calculator) ÷ CO₂ absorbed per tree

Planting Best Practices

  • Diversity: Do not plant just one species; a mix of Neem and Amaltas improves urban biodiversity.
  • Placement: Plant on the western side of buildings to block the hot afternoon sun.
  • Maintenance: Local Karachi soil needs deep watering twice a week for young saplings.
