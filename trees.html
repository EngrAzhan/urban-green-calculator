Urban Trees & Carbon Sequestration

This page summarizes tree species and sequestration values compiled from local studies (Bano et al., Shehzad et al., Pakistan Journal of Botany).

SpeciesGrowth TypeCO₂ absorbed (kg/year)
Azadirachta indica (Neem)Fast-growing30–35

Cassia fistula (Amaltas)Medium20–25
Delonix regia (Gulmohar)Medium18–22
Conocarpus erectusShrub-like10–15

How to compute trees needed

Number of trees = Annual CO₂ emissions (kg) / CO₂ absorbed per tree (kg/year). Use the "Annual CO₂ saved" number from the calculator result to estimate planting needs.

Recommendations

  • Plant a mix of native species (Neem, Peepal, Amaltas) for high sequestration and biodiversity.
  • Ensure watering and protection during the first 2 years.
  • Prefer off-road planting where survival and growth rates are higher.
Data sources: Bano et al., Shehzad et al., local studies.
