Project Overview

This website presents a step-by-step calculator to estimate diesel fuel consumption, CO₂ emissions and savings from solar. It also includes local literature-based recommendations for tree species to mitigate remaining emissions in Karachi.

  • Calculator: 3-step interactive tool.
  • Carbon Theory: Simple equations and sources.
  • Trees: Species and sequestration tables drawn from local studies.

The Hidden Cost of Our Power Backup

Every liter of diesel burned in Karachi’s generators pumps nearly 2.7kg of CO₂ and harmful particulate matter into the local breathing zone.

London is a pioneer in tackling the matter through enforcing strict emission standards on all construction and backup generators.

Turning Karachi’s Heat into Help

With over five hours of peak sunlight daily, solar energy can effectively "turn off" thousands of generators, stopping pollution at the source.

Freiburg, often called Germany’s Solar City, integrated solar PV into the very fabric of residential architecture. The city uses a "PlusEnergy" model where homes actually produce more energy than they consume.

Nature’s Own Air Purifiers

Native trees like the Neem and Peepal act as natural vacuum cleaners, capturing soot while providing the critical "Natural AC" effect.

Medellín serves as the ultimate example of "Nature’s Own Air Purifiers" through its Green Corridors (Corredores Verdes) project. The city planted thousands of trees and tropical plants along 30 major thoroughfares to create cool air rivers.
